go2mountains

我們為什麼開始 go2mountains

2022 · 回到吉爾吉斯結婚

那段時間，是我們人生中最辛苦、也最誠實的時刻。回到鄉下，在大山前完成婚禮，挑戰傳統，也面對了來自父親家族的反對與質疑。

疫情的兩年

被卡在泰國整整兩年，拍影片、剪影片、幾乎沒有收入；即使如此，我們仍然選擇繼續分享吉爾吉斯，因為那是頻道的初衷。

在山與家人之間

沒有排場，只有山、家人、腳下的土地，卻留下了深刻的記憶，也讓我們更確信要真誠記錄這片土地。

沒有商業計畫，只有真心

那時沒有旅行社的藍圖，沒有商業模式；只想用最真實、不修飾的方式，把我們熱愛的吉爾吉斯介紹給世界。

go2mountains 的成長

走過不確定與低潮，我們明白這條路是在傳遞生活方式、價值與信任。go2mountains 就這樣慢慢長出來。

Bekruby Prologue

We didn’t choose Kyrgyzstan — Kyrgyzstan caught us first

Some places never appear in your plans—not because they’re far, but because, when you’re unprepared, they call you back with something that feels like fate. For us, that place is Kyrgyzstan.

Bek’s story begins with leaving. A riding accident at age ten put him in a hospital bed for a year; while the mountain horses ran free, he relearned to walk, endured pain, and chased after missed lessons. After recovering, he wasn’t as free as before. Friends saw him differently, and he wondered if he’d ever return to being that running kid. So he chose the hardest language, the farthest road—a scholarship to Shanghai for six and a half years—a time of growth, and of escape.
