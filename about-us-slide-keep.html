Who We Are

Hi! I’m Bek, born and raised in Kyrgyzstan, a land of breathtaking mountains and rich nomadic culture. After dreaming of big cities as a child, I worked hard to earn a scholarship to study abroad, spending 10 years in cities like Shanghai, Bangkok, and Taipei. Over time, I realized that nothing compares to the beauty of nature and my homeland. In Shanghai, I met my wife, Ruby, who is from Taiwan and shares my love for nature and simple living. Together, we set out on a mission to introduce Kyrgyzstan to the world.

Bek and Ruby

2018

Meeting Bek in Shanghai opened the door to Central Asia for me. I experienced so many firsts—riding a horse, staying in a yurt, hiking mountains, and soaking in hot springs. Bek, born in the land farthest from the sea, has always loved swimming and dreams of living on a tropical island.

Bek lockdown in Thailand

2020

When COVID struck, Bek found himself lonely and stranded in a foreign land. What was supposed to be a one-week trip to Thailand turned into two and a half years. It felt as though the world had left him behind, prompting him to rethink the meaning of life.

Proposal

2021

After a year apart, Ruby went through great effort to prepare the necessary documents to come to Thailand. Bek proposed on the cliffs of Railay, one of the world's top rock climbing destinations—it seemed impossible to say no to this marriage!

Island life

2021-2022

We started filming for YouTube, learning and exploring everything from scratch on our own. Settling on a small island in Thailand for a year helped us discover the best and healthiest way of life!

Marriage.

2022

As the world began to recover, Taiwan remained closed. We returned to Kyrgyzstan to get married. Instead of hosting a banquet at a fancy hotel in the capital, we decided to overcome all obstacles and hold a traditional wedding full of memories in Beike's rural hometown where he was born!

Promoting Kyrgyzstan

2023

Our dream was to promote Kyrgyzstan, but at one point, we almost gave up and considered returning to our previous jobs. Then we realized that our friend Navat, who had been promoting Kyrgyzstan for years, was a beloved and passionate tour guide. We decided to join forces and became partners, founding a travel agency for Kyrgyzstan together.

Expressing gratitude

2023

In our first year, we hosted three groups of guests from Hong Kong and Taiwan. We are incredibly grateful for your trust and support! We never imagined that we could create and sustain a job we truly love!

Bek's message

When I was a child, I experienced two accidents that left me spending a long time in the hospital. It was then that I realized how short life is—we are not here on this Earth forever. I deeply cherish every moment I’ve had to explore the world since surviving those incidents. Don’t stop dreaming. Never stop chasing your dreams with all your heart. I am so grateful to be living in my dream right now!
